About Us

The Walt Disney Company, commonly known as Disney, is a multinational entertainment and media conglomerate headquartered in Burbank, California, USA. It was founded on October 16, 1923, by brothers Walt Disney and Roy O. Disney. Disney is one of the world's leading and most recognized entertainment companies, known for its iconic characters, films, theme parks, and diverse media offerings.
